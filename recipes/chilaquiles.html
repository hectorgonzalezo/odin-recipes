Chilaquiles

Description

The original recipe from where the very popular spin-off "Nachos" emerged.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Tomato or Tomatillo
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chili pepper
  • Queso fresco
  • Sour Cream
  • Fried egg (optional)
  • Chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil or roast the Tomato or Tomatillo
  2. Slightly fry the Onion and Garlic
  3. Blend everything with the Chili Pepper
  4. Serve on Tortilla chips
  5. Accompany with Queso freaco, Sour Cream, and other optional ingredients
